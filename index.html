How To Play:

1. Place your ships on the grid. You can flip the ships to better fit your placement style.
2. Once your ships are placed, pick a spot on the grid where the enemies ship might be.
3. You both have the same ships (destroyer, submarine, cruiser, battleship, carrier).
4. If you hit a ship try to pick a spot close to the area you hit to sink all the ships.
5. Sink the enemies ship before they sink all of yours!
